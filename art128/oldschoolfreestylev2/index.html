History

Bob Haro, the founder of BMX Freestyle was born in Pasadena, California during the 1950s. He grew up influenced by the skateboard and BMX racing. Haro was the most important early innovators of BMX Freestyle back in the 1970s. Many of his fans called him the “Godfather of BMX Freestyle”

Events

BMX Freestyling Lessons/Maintenance or Repair will be held every Sunday from 2 pm to 6 pm. To reserve a spot, please contact any of our instructors a week before your desired lesson day.

About

Ryan Tanaka, an BMX instructor and founder behind the service, was born and raised in Honolulu, Hawaii. His passion for BMX Freestyle has grew over time ever since he was 16 years old. Ryan wanted to share that same passion by teaching others. That includes learning tricks, basic bike safety and Bike Maintenance.
